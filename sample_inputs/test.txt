SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement is entered into between ABC Technologies Pvt Ltd
and XYZ Solutions on 01 January 2025.

1. Scope of Services
The Service Provider shall provide software development and maintenance
services to the Client as per agreed timelines and deliverables.

2. Payment Terms
The Client shall pay INR 5,00,000 within 30 days of invoice.
Any delay in payment shall attract a penalty of 2% per month.

This Agreement shall be valid for a period of 2 years.
The Client may terminate this Agreement at any time without cause
by providing 15 days written notice.

4. Indemnity
The Service Provider shall indemnify and hold harmless the Client
against all losses, damages, and liabilities arising from the services.

5. Confidentiality
Both parties shall maintain confidentiality of all business information
during and after the term of this Agreement.

6. Arbitration and Jurisdiction
Any dispute arising out of this Agreement shall be resolved through
arbitration in Mumbai in accordance with Indian laws.

7. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with
the laws of India.
