EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is entered into between ABC Technologies Pvt Ltd
and Mr. Rahul Kumar on 01 February 2025.

1. Appointment and Duties
The Employee shall perform duties assigned by the Company and shall comply
with all company policies and instructions.

2. Compensation
The Company shall pay the Employee a monthly salary of INR 50,000.
Any breach of company policy shall attract a penalty as decided by management.

3. Working Hours
The Employee shall work for a minimum of 9 hours per day and may be required
to work additional hours without extra compensation.

4. Termination
The Company may terminate this Agreement at any time without notice.
The Employee shall not terminate employment during the first 2 years.

5. Confidentiality and Intellectual Property
The Employee shall maintain confidentiality of all company information.
All intellectual property created during employment shall belong exclusively
to the Company.

6. Indemnity
The Employee shall indemnify the Company against any loss arising from
misconduct or negligence.

7. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.
